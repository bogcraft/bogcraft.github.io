BogCraft Rules

Please make sure you follow this

1

No cheating of any kind if u have been caught cheating with: E.G: X-ray, duping, exploiting, boat fly etc if caught you could deal with a ban.

2

No use of mods/resource packs that give a significant advantage over others.

This includes (but is not limited to) hacks,baritone, player health/armor viewing mods and resource packs that make textures abnormally large so they are more visible. However, performance mods, fullbright and xaeros world map are allowed.

3

No griefing.

This includes griefing terrain around builds and killing pets/animals/villagers.

4

No stealing.

5


PvP and wars are allowed.

However, they should be done within reason. If the game becomes unplayable for the other party then you will be asked to stop. Explosives, combat logging and spawn trapping are not allowed. ( if in combat u CANNOT use the command /home whatsoever! Once safely out of combat then u may use that command) also if u kill a player you cannot take anything from the killed player

6

No more than 2 chunk loaders per player.

They must be easily toggleable. If they are causing too much lag you may be asked to turn them off.

7

No lag machines.

It can cause to slow down the server.

8

 

If the player fails to follow these rules they may warned first, then a ban may occur.
